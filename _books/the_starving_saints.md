This book has an aptly slow start: a beleagured populace is under prolonged siege in Aymar Castle. Hope is all but lost until it isn't. Divine figures of the Constant Lady and her Saints arrive, and then everyone is enthralled by them.

Ser Voyne and the Constant Lady, Phosyne, a heretical sorcerer/alchemist need to figure out the mystery behind the sudden turn of luck. There is also a serving girl named Treila who harbors a secret hatred for Ser Voyne, but also a desire to escape the castle walls. 

So far the characters are quite memorable. I hope the pace quickens.
