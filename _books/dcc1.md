These my notes and quotes from the book since there is a lot to keep straight. <b>Spoilers ahead!</b>
<br>
<br>
In Dungeon Crawler Carl, Carl and his ex-girlfriend's cat, Donut, enter an <b>18-Level World Dungeon</b>. This dungeon was created by the "Kua-Tin, the dominant species of the Borant System and principal owners of the Borant Corporation" who resent the term "mud skipper". Quickly upon entering the dungeon, Carl meets his soon-to-be manager, Mordecai, who was "born a skyfowl, but ... became a Changeling when [he] reached floor three of the dungeon."
<br>
<br>
Donut gets the Enchanted Crown of the Sepsis Whore in her Platinum Quadruped Box, but placing this crown upon your head permanently places you within the royal line of succession for the Blood Sultanate on the ninth floor of the world dungeon... Royal members of the Blood Sultanate will be required to slay the Sultan and all other members of the royal family before descending to the tenth floor.
<br>
<br>
They run into some adversaires in the form of Crawler <b>Frank Q.</b>, who murdered Crawler Rebecca W.
<br>
<br>
They hear about another fellow crawler, <b>Lucia Mar</b>, who is "a girl about 13 years old with a pair of rottweilers", named Cici and Gustavo 3.
<br>
<br>
They team up with the <b>Meadow Lark Party, a group of elderly folks and their caretakers, including Brandon, Chris, Yolanda (CNA), and Imani (another nursing assitant)</b>. "Brandon and his brother ... were the maintenance guys."
<br>
<br>
Carl and Donut end up on a dungeon talk show hosted by <b>Odette</b>, "a naked crab-taur wearing a bug mask. The lower body of the woman was entirely crab. Black and red with a lumpy shell and multiple, chitinous legs. The shape and makeup of the shell was that of a king crab, but the size of a brown bear. Her body seemed completely separate from the rest of her form. The legs crowded one another, unable to tuck themselves underneath the desk. The legs seemed to twitch on their own accord." Her "triangular bug head was black with mirrored, compound eyes the size of footballs. Twin antennae spread from her head with a span over at least six feet, as wide as the crab body." But it turns out that "That entire getup was [her] armor when I was like [Donut and Carl]. It was what [she] was wearing when [she] reached the stairwell for the <b>thirteenth floor</b> in a season long before Mordecai's as she was his trainer.

They are assigned a low-ranking emploee from the Borant Corporation named <b>Zev</b>.
<blockquote>
    "Zev looked like an astronaut from a 1950s sci-fi comic. She wore a round, glass helmet filled with water. A pair of tubes snaked from the back of the helmet to a bulky backpack. The water bubbled like she was in a portable fish tank. The rest of her humanoid fish body was hidden inside of a white, mesh spacesuit. The whole getup was half space-age, half old-school deep diving suit."
</blockquote>
<b>What we know about the floors:</b>
<br>
There are <b>18 floors</b>. "additional rules come into play once any crawlers descend to the tenth floor." The furthest anyone has gotten was <b>floor 13</b>, but "he died within a half-hour of hitting the floor." Carl and Donut are vaguely told “It’s not worth it, no matter what they tell you. Not until <b>floor 12</b>, and even then, negotiate as much as you can. Remember that.” "every three floors are the same setting. Sort of. The third, sixth, ninth and so forth are all linked in a way that will later become clear.”

<b>What we know about the world:</b>
<br>
This world is godless: "just so you know, Grull isn’t a real god. There are no real gods in this game."
<br>
"[Borant doesn't] get that money, or any money, from the advertising or the tourism funds or elite monster sponsorship or anything else until the season is over." "Borant is being forced by their own government to end the game as quickly as possible."
<br>
[The Valtay are] a corporate system government, and they’re the ones who currently have an entire fleet parked outside of the Borant system, ready to collect on the debt [that Borant owes]. They were hours away from initiating a full-scale collection action when the kua-tin stopped them in their tracks by starting the season.
