I'm a huge Joe Abercrombie fan, so I was terribly dissappointed by this book. The First Law trilogy is likely my favorite series of all time. However, I'm not a fan of pulpy books, and something about his characterizations in this book, which I normally feel are in a league of their own, fell flat for me. The characters didn't undergo much development, and I didn't really feel invested in many of them. This book just missed the mark for me, which is really sad because I eagerly awaited this book's release.
